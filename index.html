INFO

  • Click the kitty to gain kitties at the rate given by Kittens per Click (KPC)
  • You gain kitties passively at the rate given by Kittens per Second (KPS)
  • Purchase 'items' to increase your KPC & KPS
  • Purchase upgrades to make those 'items' gain you more kitties!
  • To buy an upgrade, you have to have less items than your current upgrades for that item.
  • Kitten: These cute little babies increases your KPS by 1 per Kitten.
  • Noodle: This guy's last brain cell increases your KPS by 3 per second.
  • Chonker: This hefty chonk makes your clicks heavier, increasing your KPC by 5.
  • Catnip: Kittens have infinite energy, but a kitten on catnip breaks the laws of physics, increasing their KPS bonus by an extra 1!
  • 2nd Dinner: Feed your chonk some 2nd dinner and increase their KPC bonus by another 5!
